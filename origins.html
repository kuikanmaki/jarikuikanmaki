About the Book

A True Crime Investigation into the Cover-Up of the Century

For years, people all over the world were told that the laboratory origin of SARS-CoV-2 was a dangerous conspiracy theory — and that the virus was of natural origin.

But now, it is time for the truth.

In January 2020, journalist Jari Kuikanmäki began investigating emerging reports of a novel virus in Wuhan, China — and an explosive theory that it may have originated not in nature, but in a laboratory.

What began as a news story turned into a five-year global investigation — unearthing a tangled web of bioweapons research, scientific cover-ups, government corruption, and clandestine operations at the highest levels of power.

What was the United States' role in the controversial Wuhan research? What does the evidence truly reveal about the virus's origins? Was this the result of a natural spillover — or something far more sinister?

A gripping blend of thriller, investigative journalism, and scientific detective work, Origins of Coronavirus is a definitive, meticulously researched account that unravels the greatest mystery — and potential crime — of our time.

Backed by hundreds of sources and a thorough examination of the available evidence, Origins of Coronavirus is essential reading for anyone who wants to know how the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic truly began — and how those in power tried to silence the truth.

About the Author

Jari Kuikanmäki

Jari Kuikanmäki is an investigative journalist, podcast host, and producer with a background in Finnish national television. He holds a BSc in Political Science from the University of Helsinki and an MSc in Media and Communications from the London School of Economics.

Origins of Coronavirus is his first book.
