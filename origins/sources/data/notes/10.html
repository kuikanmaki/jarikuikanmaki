Fact-Checking Reversals
For example, in September 2020 the fact-checking service PolitiFact declared lab-origin claims false, stating that “the genetic structure of the novel coronavirus … rules out the possibility that it was manipulated in a lab.” In May 2021 the outlet added an editor’s note acknowledging that the question remained under scientific debate:

Tucker Carlson guest airs debunked conspiracy theory that coronavirus was created in a lab, PolitiFact, Editor’s Note, May 17, 2021.

In reality, the genetic data had not at any point allowed such a definitive conclusion.
