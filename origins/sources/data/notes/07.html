Notes to Chapter 7

Private Discussions Among Proximal Origin Authors

The authors of the influential “Proximal Origin” paper privately discussed serial passaging as a possible explanation for the virus’s features. For more information, see Chapter 6 and Emily Kopp’s detailed timeline in Chapter 6 Sources (Kopp 2023).

WHO Advising Against Travel Restrictions

The chapter also notes that the World Health Organization continued to advise against travel restrictions even as the virus spread internationally. For example, WHO’s Situation Report 39 (February 28, 2020) stated that “WHO has published updated advice for international traffic, and continues to recommend against the application of any travel or trade restrictions in relation to the current COVID-19 outbreak.”
