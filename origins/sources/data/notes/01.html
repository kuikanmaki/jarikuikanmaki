Notes to Chapter 1

The virus was initially designated “2019-nCoV” (novel coronavirus 2019). It received its official name, SARS-CoV-2, on 11 February 2020.

Key Figure: Edward Holmes

The Australian virologist who first published the SARS-CoV-2 genome outside China plays a significant role later in the origin investigation. He was one of the co-authors of the influential “Proximal Origin of SARS-CoV-2” paper (see Chapter 6 Sources: Andersen et al., 2020). For later revelations concerning his collaborations with Wuhan Institute of Virology scientists and questions surrounding the publication of key virus genomes, see Chapter 19.
