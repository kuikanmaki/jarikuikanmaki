About the Book

A True Crime Investigation into the Cover-Up of the Century

For years, people all over the world were told that the laboratory origin of SARS-CoV-2 was nothing more than a dangerous conspiracy theory — and that the virus was of natural origin.

But now, it is time for the truth.

In January 2020, journalist Jari Kuikanmäki began investigating emerging reports of a novel virus in Wuhan, China — and an explosive theory, that the virus may have originated not in nature, but inside a laboratory.

What began as a news story turned into a six-year global investigation — unearthing a tangled web of hidden military-linked research, scientific cover-ups, government corruption, and clandestine operations at the highest levels of power.

How deep was the U.S. government’s involvement in the dangerous research conducted in Wuhan? Why were early warnings about a possible lab origin ignored or dismissed? And how was the truth about the pandemic’s origins systematically concealed from the public?

A gripping blend of investigative journalism, scientific analysis, and political thriller, Origins of Coronavirus is a meticulously researched account that unravels one of the greatest mysteries — and potential crimes — of our time.

Backed by hundreds of sources and a thorough examination of the available evidence, Origins of Coronavirus is essential reading for anyone who wants to know how the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic truly began — and how those in power tried to silence the truth.
